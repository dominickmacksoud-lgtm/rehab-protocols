For Licensed Healthcare Professionals Only

RehabProtocols.com catalogs rehabilitation protocols sourced from third-party academic medical centers, hospital systems, and orthopedic specialists. This site is intended exclusively for use by licensed healthcare professionals — physical therapists, athletic trainers, physician assistants, and surgeons.

This site is not intended for patients or the general public.

All content is provided for clinical reference and educational purposes only. Protocol summaries may not reflect the most current version of a source document. Nothing on this site constitutes medical advice, and use of this site does not create a clinician-patient relationship.

The operative surgeon's postoperative instructions and the original source protocol always take precedence over any summary on this site. Always verify the currency and accuracy of any protocol before clinical application.
